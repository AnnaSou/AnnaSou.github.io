Before we change columns' names, let us explain their meanings. It was found that happiness can be explained by the following six factor: GDP per capita is self explonatory Social support, measured by having someone to count on in times of troubles Healthy life expectancy - healthy years of life expectancy Perceptions of corruption is meauserd by a percieved absence of corruption in goverment and business Freedom to make life choices Generosity as measured by recent donations

Taken together these six variables explained up to 3/4 of the variation in national annual average ladder score among countries using data for the years 2005 to 2016.
